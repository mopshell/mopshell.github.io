Bio

I am a Data Science Advisor at LENWELL International Previously, I worked in Chevron as both a staff research geophysicist and principal data scientist. During my time in industry I contributed to the development of geophysical technologies aimed at identifying new discoveries as well as increasing hydrocarbon recovery of existing reservoirs. I have also been involved in the enhancement of beam fidelity project, computational accleration of seismic data processing and Deep learning/Deep reinforcement learning for oilfield development optimization.

I earned a PhD in Geophysics from the University of Texas at Austin as part of the Institute for Geophysics at UT Austin under the supervision of Prof. Paul Stoffa. My research contributions spanned across the fields of Stochastic tomography, seismic imaging, and inversion through the development of novel methods aimed at using Gaussian beams to improve the quality and resolution of subsurface imaging products.
